Mathematical Background
The muon corresponds to the Mandelbrot parameter c_μ = -1.7549 + 0i. The Nash-4 regularization introduces a convergence radius r₄ ≈ 0.002, displacing the parameter to c_corr = -1.7529 + 0i.
The asymptotic value is:
z_∞(c) = -1/2 + i√(r₄ · |c + 0.75|)

The anomalous magnetic moment correction is:
Δa_μ = α · Im[z_(c_corr) - z_∞(c_μ)]
